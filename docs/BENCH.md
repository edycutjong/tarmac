## How to read it

- **Protected pax stranded** counts passengers with a regulated need (MED / UM /
  WCHR / tight connection) whose SLA the final manifest failed. Seat *supply* is
  physically fixed (139 legal seats for 180 passengers), so raw strandings barely
  move — the society's win is in *who* flies, which this row makes explicit.
- **Society − mediator** shows the mediator is load-bearing, not decorative:
  without adjudication, contested claims are quarantined at the round cap and the
  society ends up *worse* than a single planner on throughput.
- **Crew duty violations** is a tested invariant (I2): the single planner presses
  the duty-illegal ferry into service; both society conditions refuse it.
